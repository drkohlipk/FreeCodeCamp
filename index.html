Kurt Cobain's Life in a Nutshell:

  • 1967 - Kurt Donald Cobain born to Wendy and Donald Cobain
  • 1981 - Kurt gets his first guitar
  • 1985 - Kurt drops out of high school
  • 1988 - Nirvana records demo with Dale Crover
  • 1988 - Kurt smashes his first guitar
  • 1988 - Love Buzz/Big Cheese single released
  • 1989 - After recording Bleach, they do a short west coast tour
  • 1989 - Bleach released
  • 1989 - Blew EP recorded
  • 1989 - Nirvana plays first European show, Newcastle, England
  • 1990 - Sliver single released
  • 1991 - Studio session: recorded Anuerysm and Even In His Youth
  • 1991 - Nirvana first plays Smells Like Teen Spirit
  • 1991 - Nirvana formally signs with Geffen Records
  • 1991 - Started recording Nevermind
  • 1991 - The video for Smells Like Teen Spirit is made
  • 1991 - Nevermind is released, debuted at #144 on Billboard
  • 1992 - Nirvana plays in the MTV studios
  • 1992 - Nirvana plays Saturday Night Live, Nevermind hits #1
  • 1992 - Nirvana begins a world tour
  • 1992 - Come As You Are video is made
  • 1992 - Kurt Cobain marries Courtney Love in Waikiki, Hawaii
  • 1992 - Lithium single released with Nevermind lyric sheet
  • 1992 - Frances Bean Cobain is born
  • 1992 - Nirvana plays Lithium at MTV music awards, wins 2 awards
  • 1992 - In Bloom Video hits MTV
  • 1992 - Incesticide released
  • 1993 - In Utero recorded in 2 weeks
  • 1993 - Sliver video hits MTV
  • 1993 - Nirvana wins Best Alternative Video award at MTV Video awards
  • 1993 - In Utero released in UK
  • 1993 - Heart Shaped Box video premieres
  • 1993 - After a week delay, In Utero released in US
  • 1993 - Nirvana begins tour supporting In Utero
  • 1993 - Nirvana records Unplugged session for MTV
  • 1993 - All Apologies video hits MTV
  • 1993 - Nirvana Unplugged airs on MTV
  • 1993 - MTV's New Years Eve special airs, Nirvana headlines
  • 1994 - Kurt Cobain is hospitalized comatose
  • 1994 - Kurt awakens from coma, asks for milkshake
  • 1994 - Kurt leaves hospital under his own power
  • 1994 - Kurt "commits suicide." Kurt Donald Cobain 1967-1994
